Our purpose

We are a comminuted or a business network located in Bilbao- Getxo Spain is to further the interests of businesses. The member are citizens who want to improve her business the community chamber of the commerce acts as a spokesman for the business who convert the group thinking into action and promote a specific product or services type that can be more beneficial for the local businesses associate to this site.

Our goals

Promote with responsibility,confidence and professional methods to improve our business network.
Promote our business wealth as a city.
Help our communited to improve their business with professional techniques.
